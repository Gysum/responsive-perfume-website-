A Legacy Built
on Passion

Founded in the heart of Paris in 2001, Maison Élixir began as a small atelier dedicated to the art of perfumery. Our master perfumers source only the finest ingredients — from Grasse jasmine to Indian sandalwood — to create compositions that resonate with the soul.

Every bottle is a testament to decades of expertise, meticulous attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

  • Ethically sourced rare ingredients from 30+ countries
  • Hand-blended by master perfumers with 25+ years experience
  • Eco-conscious packaging & cruelty-free formulations
  • Bespoke fragrance consultation available worldwide
